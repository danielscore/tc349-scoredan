Quiz:
1. True or False. A Michigan LLC can have one or multiple member s/partners.
	True

2. True or False. Once you have a business name, the next step is to open a bank account.
	False, you will need your EIN first, to open a bank account

3. True or False. You only have to file a Michigan LLC Annual Report if your company earned money during the fiscal year.
	False

4. Name one thing general liability insurance might cover.
	Any personal injury that happens on your premises such as slipping on ice

5. True or False. The City of Lansing levies an income tax.
	True

6. Calculate your hourly billing rate for a web design business if you aim to pay yourself $45,000/year with a profit margin of 25%.
	Labor: $45,000 * 1.35 =  $60750/1152 hours = $52.73 ~ $53/hour
	Overhead: $12,000/1152 hours = $11/hour
	Operating Cost: $64/hour
	Profit: $64/hour * .25 = $16/hour
	Final Hourly Rate: $80/hour



7. True or False. Work for hire means that you get to remain the copyright holder after you've delivered your work to the client.
	False

9. True or False. You can use your personal checking account for business as long as you are not a corporation.
	False, if you have an LLC, you should set up a separate bank account so you can track business expenses
